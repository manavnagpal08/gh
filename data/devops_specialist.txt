Job Title: DevOps Specialist

About Us:
We are a leading company in software delivery and cloud infrastructure looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to accelerate software delivery through automation and robust infrastructure.

The Role:
As a DevOps Specialist, you will be responsible for automating CI/CD pipelines and deployment processes, managing and optimizing cloud infrastructure as code, and implementing monitoring, logging, and alerting solutions. You will work closely with development and operations teams to improve system reliability, scalability, and efficiency. This role requires a strong commitment to automation and operational excellence.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design, implement, and maintain robust CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins, GitLab CI, or GitHub Actions.
- Provision and manage cloud infrastructure on AWS, Azure, or GCP using Terraform and Ansible.
- Implement and manage containerization technologies like Docker and Kubernetes.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, DevOps, or Software Engineering or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in DevOps Specialist or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in CI/CD, Docker, Kubernetes, Cloud Platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP).
- Demonstrated experience with Terraform, Ansible, Python/Bash Scripting, Linux System Administration.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack, Git, Microservices Architecture.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- driving continuous innovation and operational stability.
